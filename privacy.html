Privacy Policy

Speak Like Yourself in Portuguese · Last updated: 2026-07-10

This Privacy Policy explains how Speak Like Yourself in Portuguese (the "Service") handles data. In this policy, "the Service" refers to the website at sonderrift.github.io/speak-like-yourself and its associated application, which promote Brazilian Portuguese lessons offered on Preply and provide free learning materials.

Data the Service collects

The Service collects no personal data from visitors. It has no accounts, no forms, no newsletter, no cookies set by the Service, and no analytics trackers. Downloading the free PDF requires no signup and transmits no information to the Service.

Third-party services

The website is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may log standard technical data (such as IP addresses) as part of operating the service; see GitHub's privacy statement. Fonts are served by Google Fonts. Outbound links lead to Preply and Pinterest, each of which maintains its own privacy policy.

Use of the Pinterest API

The Service uses the Pinterest API. The Pinterest API is used solely to publish the Service's own educational content (Pins) to the Service's own Pinterest account and boards. The Service does not access, collect, or process data about other Pinterest users.

The Service is not endorsed by, affiliated with, sponsored by, or otherwise associated with Pinterest. "Pinterest" is a trademark of its respective owner.

Handling of Pinterest data

The only Pinterest-derived data the Service retains is the authorization token that permits publishing on the Service's own behalf, together with references to the Pins it has created. When the Pinterest account is disconnected from the Service, the associated authorization token is revoked and any Pinterest-derived data held by the Service is deleted.

The Service does not sell, resell, redistribute, or otherwise share Pinterest content or any Pinterest-derived data with third parties.
