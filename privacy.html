Who we are

This website (sonderrift.github.io/speak-like-yourself) promotes Brazilian Portuguese lessons taught by Rafael on Preply and offers free learning materials.

What data we collect

None. This site has no accounts, no forms, no newsletter, no cookies set by us, and no analytics trackers. Downloading the free PDF requires no signup and sends us no information.

Third parties

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may log standard technical data (such as IP addresses) as part of operating the service; see GitHub's privacy statement. Fonts are served by Google Fonts. Links on this site lead to Preply and Pinterest, which have their own privacy policies.

Use of the Pinterest API

This site and its associated application use the Pinterest API. We use the Pinterest API solely to publish our own educational content (Pins) to our own Pinterest account and boards. We do not access, collect, or process data about other Pinterest users.

This site and application are not endorsed by, affiliated with, sponsored by, or otherwise associated with Pinterest. "Pinterest" is a trademark of its respective owner.

Pinterest data handling

The only Pinterest-derived data we hold is the authorization token that lets our application publish on our own behalf, plus references to the Pins we have created. If and when the Pinterest account is disconnected from our application, the associated authorization token is revoked and any Pinterest-derived data we hold is deleted from our systems.

We do not sell, resell, redistribute, or otherwise share Pinterest content or any Pinterest-derived data with third parties.
