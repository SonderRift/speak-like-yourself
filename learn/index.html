Quick answers

What exactly do I get, and how?

A digital download you keep forever: the full workbook in dark and light editions plus the audio pack. Checkout is handled by Payhip and the files are yours to download instantly, no subscription and no account to manage.

Is this for complete beginners?

Yes. Complete beginners are exactly who the survival layer is built for, so you can function in Portuguese long before you feel "ready".

Is the audio a real human voice?

The audio is generated with a high-quality Brazilian Portuguese text-to-speech voice, at three speeds (normal, slow, and slower) so you can train your ear. It is clear and natural, and it is not a claim of a human recording.

Do I need to take lessons to use it?

No. The kits are fully self-study and stand on their own. If you ever want live practice that is a separate choice, not something you need to buy here.
